Privacy Policy

How We Protect
Your Data

GSN — the world's leading business referral network — is committed to safeguarding every member's and visitor's information with transparency, care, and respect across our global platform.

Last updated: June 2025  ·  Applies to all GSN platforms and chapters worldwide

📋

Information We Collect

When you visit a GSN chapter, register on our platform, or contact us, we may collect:

  • Full name, email address, and phone number
  • Business name, industry category, and professional background
  • Referral activity, meeting attendance, and platform usage data
  • Device and browser information for analytics purposes

We only collect information that is necessary to operate chapter meetings, manage memberships, and improve the GSN platform.

🎯

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Facilitate referrals and business introductions within GSN chapters
  • Improve AI-powered matchmaking and member recommendations
  • Send chapter updates, event invitations, and network communications
  • Maintain the integrity and accountability of the referral system
  • Comply with legal obligations where applicable

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party, ever.

🔒

Data Security

GSN implements industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Access controls limiting data to authorised personnel only
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments

While no system is 100% impenetrable, we are committed to promptly addressing any breach and notifying affected members as required by law.

🍪

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to remember your preferences, analyse traffic patterns, and improve your experience. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may limit some functionality.

We use anonymised analytics tools (such as page view counters) and do not use cross-site tracking or fingerprinting technologies.

✅

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — update any inaccurate or outdated information
  • Deletion — request that your data be erased from our systems
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection — opt out of certain processing activities

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through the Contact page and we will respond within 30 days.

📬

Contact & Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. We will notify active members of any material changes via email or platform notification. Continued use of GSN services following an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

For privacy-related questions or requests, please reach out via our Contact page.
